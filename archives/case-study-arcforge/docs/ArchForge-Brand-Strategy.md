# ArchForge.au Brand Strategy & Style Guide
**Version 1.0 | November 6, 2025**

---

## Brand Foundation

### Mission
Forge AI-powered efficiency for Australian SMBs through private, intelligent automation—starting with bookkeeping, scaling to voice-integrated business agents.

### Vision
Every Australian small business has access to enterprise-grade AI that feels like magic, not code.

### Brand Promise
**"AI That Builds Your Empire"** — We transform administrative burden into strategic advantage through playful, powerful automation.

### Target Audience
Australian small-to-medium businesses (<10 employees) in:
- Finance & bookkeeping firms
- Dental practices
- Construction companies
- Any SMB drowning in admin work (60% report struggles)

**Psychographics:**
- Ambitious but time-strapped
- Wary of complex tech but curious about AI
- Value ROI over features
- Want to focus on growth, not grunt work

---

## Brand Personality

### Core Traits
1. **Trustworthy Craftsman** — Anvil-solid reliability meets artisan attention to detail
2. **Playful Alchemist** — Joyful surprise ("magic wand" moments) without intimidating complexity
3. **Australian Pragmatist** — No-nonsense value, relatable banter, clear facts over hype
4. **Empowering Builder** — Tools that make users feel like architects of their future

### Voice & Tone Matrix

| Context | Voice Style | Example |
|---------|-------------|---------|
| **Hero/Headers** | Inspiring, aspirational | "Forge Your Future" |
| **Value Props** | Playful, benefit-focused | "AI handles the grunt work—you dream the dreams" |
| **Features** | Clean, fact-driven | "40% faster Xero reconciliation. Guaranteed." |
| **CTAs** | Urgent, action-oriented | "Forge It Now" (not "Learn More") |
| **Social/Content** | Witty AU banter + helpful | "Your invoices are lonely. Let's get them organized." |

**Tonal Guidelines:**
- ✅ Use: Australian idioms ("no worries", "fair dinkum"), second-person ("you"), active voice
- ❌ Avoid: Corporate jargon, passive voice, over-promising ("revolutionize", "game-changer")

## Visual Identity System

### Color Palette

#### Primary Colors
- **Forge Black** `#1A1A1A` — 70% usage
  - Authority, sophistication, depth
  - Use: Backgrounds, primary text, logo base

- **Spark Orange** `#FF6B35` — 20% usage
  - Energy, urgency, transformation
  - Use: CTAs, accent elements, hover states, "spark" animations

- **Vector Blue** `#4A90E2` — 10% usage
  - Trust, calm intelligence, AI/tech
  - Use: Icons, data visualizations, secondary elements

#### Supporting Palette
- **Anvil Grey** `#6B6B6B` — Body text, subtle backgrounds
- **Ember Glow** `#FF8C5A` — Orange tint for gradients
- **Steel White** `#F5F5F5` — Light backgrounds, cards

#### Gradients
- **Forge Glow**: Linear black (#1A1A1A) → orange (#FF6B35) at 45°
- **Depth Field**: Radial blue (#4A90E2) → black (#1A1A1A) for parallax layers

### Typography

#### Font Stack
```
Primary: Inter (Variable)
- Headings: Inter Bold 700
- Subheadings: Inter SemiBold 600
- Body: Inter Regular 400
- Captions: Inter Medium 500

Accent (CTAs/playful elements): Raleway Bold or custom "Spark Script"
```

#### Hierarchy
- **H1 (Hero)**: 56-72px, Inter Bold, arched path effect
- **H2 (Section)**: 40-48px, Inter Bold
- **H3 (Feature)**: 28-32px, Inter SemiBold
- **Body**: 16-18px, Inter Regular, 1.6 line-height
- **CTAs**: 18-20px, Raleway Bold, uppercase tracking

### Imagery & Graphics

#### Photography Style
- **Subject**: Real Australian SMB owners in authentic workspaces (not stock)
- **Treatment**: Warm, natural lighting; slight desaturation except orange/blue accents
- **Composition**: Environmental portraits showing "before chaos" → "after calm"

#### Iconography
- **Style**: Line icons (2px stroke), rounded corners
- **Motifs**: Tools (anvil, hammer, wrench) morphing into tech (nodes, circuits, vectors)
- **Animation**: Micro-interactions on hover (spark particles, glow pulses)

#### Illustration Approach
- **Hero Scene**: 3D parallax "forge journey" — Workshop anvil → AI neural network → dashboard UI
  - Layer 1 (foreground): Anvil with sparks
  - Layer 2 (midground): Hammer striking, particles forming data vectors
  - Layer 3 (background): Neural network nodes connecting to business dashboard
- **Icons/Diagrams**: Minimal line art with orange spark accents

## UI/UX Principles

### Design Philosophy
**"Effortless Depth"** — Simple surface, powerful beneath (like a good anvil)

### Layout Patterns
1. **Single-Page Scroll** — Continuous narrative journey (forge process metaphor)
2. **Parallax Layers** — Depth creates delight (Star Atlas inspiration)
3. **Fly-In Navigation** — Smooth, context-aware menu appearance
4. **Modular Cards** — Notion-style component blocks for offers/features

### Interaction Design
- **Buttons**: Orange "Forge It" primary with spark particle animation on hover
- **Forms**: Natural language inputs ("Tell us about your business...")
- **Reveals**: Progressive disclosure with fade-in + slight scale (builds reciprocity)
- **Feedback**: Toast notifications with playful copy ("Forging your request...")

### Accessibility
- WCAG 2.1 AA minimum (4.5:1 contrast)
- Keyboard navigation for all interactions
- Alt text with character (not "image_01" but "Anvil sparking transformation into AI nodes")

## Psychological Design Hooks

Based on Cialdini, Hormozi, Sutherland principles:

1. **Reciprocity** — Free audit with "magic wand" AI reveal (irrational delight)
2. **Scarcity** — "2 slots/week" for consulting tier (limited availability)
3. **Social Proof** — Pilot testimonials, "5 SMBs forged in Week 1"
4. **Authority** — ABN display, AU-specific compliance mentions
5. **Liking** — Playful AU banter, relatable business owner photos
6. **Unity** — "Fellow AU builders" language, shared growth stories
7. **Commitment** — Progressive form steps (small yeses build to big yes)

### Visual Psychology
- **Orange sparks** = Urgency prime (Prospect Theory loss aversion)
- **Blue vectors** = Trust, calm intelligence (ease tech fears)
- **Depth parallax** = Vivid visualization (Hormozi "dream outcome")
- **Anvil motif** = Fluency → Trust (familiar = safe)

## Brand Guardrails

### Always Do
✅ Lead with ROI/time savings (facts)
✅ Use Australian context (ABS data, Xero, AU SMBs)
✅ Inject playful metaphors ("forge", "sparks", "empire")
✅ Show before/after (chaos → calm)
✅ Offer guarantees (risk reversal)

### Never Do
❌ Tech jargon without plain explanation (no "RAG vectorization" alone)
❌ Overpromise ("AI will replace you" — it's a tool, not magic)
❌ US-centric examples (use AU currency, regions, norms)
❌ Boring stock photos (generic office, fake diversity poses)
❌ Apologetic tone ("maybe", "possibly" — be confident)
