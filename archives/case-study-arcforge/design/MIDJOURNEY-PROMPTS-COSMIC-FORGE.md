## üî• CORE CONCEPT: THE COSMIC FORGE

**Philosophy:**
The forge isn't an object (anvil) - it's a **process of transformation**. Raw cosmic energy and chaos coalesce into precise geometric networks and architectural structures. The iconography should show this moment of creation: nebulous particles forming into ordered nodes and connections.

**Visual Language:**
- Nebulous cosmic clouds (blue/orange/purple)
- Geometric network structures emerging
- Particle streams flowing from chaos to order
- Technical precision meeting organic energy
- "Formation in progress" aesthetic

**The "Forge Hole" Element:**
- Central point/crater/portal where energy concentrates
- Orange glowing core like molten metal or star formation
- Vortex or gravitational well pulling particles together
- Can be literal (cone/crater) or abstract (energy nexus)
